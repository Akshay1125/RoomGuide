Finding your room at Nalanda complex can be confusing initially. But in reality it is quite simple. Your room number is of the form "NX abc", where X is either A, R, or C - representing Academic, Rectangular, and Circular. The first digit represents the floor number, the second digit represents the block number, and the third digit represents the classroom.

For example, NC 341 is located on the circular block near Subway, on the third floor in the NC4 block.
